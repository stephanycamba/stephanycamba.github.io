About
My name is Stephany Camba. I live in Toronto, ON.

I'm an experimental animation student at OCAD University with interests in digital and traditional art. Although I primarily do 2D animation, I like to explore the unique capabilites of 3D animation and stop-motion.

Apart from art, I've expanded my creative capacities to website creation and dabbling in some coding.

My favourite hobby of all time would be watercolour painting, which I bring with me when I go places.
