My name is Stephany Camba. I live in Toronto, ON.

I'm an experimental animation student at OCAD University with interests in digital and traditional art.

Apart from art, I've expanded my creative capacities to website creation and dabbling in some coding.

One thing I like to do for fun is make art games that are playable on Itch.io. I enjoy making the models and map layout/ environment. My favourite hobby of all time would be watercolour painting, which I bring with me when I go places.
