Cake Rules!

My thesis film made during Fall 2023 at Ontario College of Art and Design.

About
A crafty demon who lures a child into another dimension where she has to make a decision to stay or go back home. She is presented with two cupcakes: one red and one blue. Now faced with a dilemma will she choose infinite cake and become a demon forever? or go home without a crumb?
