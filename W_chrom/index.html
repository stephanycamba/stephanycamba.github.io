Chromacity Puzzle Game


In this collaborative effort, I made the main map, the last map (yellow level), and all the 3D models seen here.

About

"A puzzle exploration game in a world void of colour. It's up to you to bring colour back to a once bright and beautiful world by solving puzzles and collecting each color's crystal."

Made in collaboration between OCADU and UofT students during winter 2023.

Play game on itch.io:https://ramdaj.itch.io/chromacity

Applications

Made using Unity game engine and Maya modeling software.
