Chromacity

In this collaborative effort I designed the game's main map, the last level, and all the models documented on this page.

About
A puzzle exploration game in a world void of colour. It's up to you to bring colour back to a once bright and beautiful world by solving puzzles and collecting each color's crystal.
Made in collaboration between OCADU and UofT students during winter 2023 for Level Up Student Showcase Toronto.
