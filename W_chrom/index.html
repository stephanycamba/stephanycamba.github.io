Chromacity Puzzle Game


These are my contributions to the game. I made the main map and the last map (yellow level).

About

"A puzzle exploration game in a world void of colour. It's up to you to bring colour back to a once bright and beautiful city by solving puzzles and collecting the each color's crystal."

Made in collaboration between OCADU and UofT students during winter 2023.

Applications

Made using Unity game engine and Maya modeling software.
